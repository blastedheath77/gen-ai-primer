Tokens: How AI Reads Text

Breaking text into pieces the model can count.

Before an LLM can process anything, text is split into tokens — small chunks that might be whole words, parts of words, or even single characters. This is how all text enters the model.

Key concepts

Tokens, not words atomic unit
Common = short, rare = split learned vocabulary
Why pricing is per-token £ × tokens
Different models, different splits no shared standard
