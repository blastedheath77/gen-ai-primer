Tokens: How AI Reads Text

Breaking text into pieces

Before an LLM can process anything, text is split into tokens — small chunks that might be whole words, parts of words, or even single characters. This is how all text enters the model.

Key Points

  • Tokens are the fundamental unit the model works with — not words, not characters
  • Common words = 1 token; rare or long words = multiple tokens
  • This is why AI pricing is per-token
  • Different models use different tokenisers
