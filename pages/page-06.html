Context Changes Everything

Same word, different meaning — it depends entirely on the neighbours.

A word in isolation is ambiguous. An LLM doesn't look up a fixed meaning — it recalculates meaning from the surrounding context. Bank near river points to one location in meaning-space; bank near money points somewhere entirely different.

Key concepts

Static → contextual meaning gets updated
Beyond keywords disambiguation built in
Order matters same words ≠ same meaning
It happens repeatedly layer after layer
Try it
Swap the surrounding words on the right and watch the focal word position in meaning-space move. The word itself never changes — only its company does.
