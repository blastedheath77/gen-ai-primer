Attention: What Relates to What

Selective focus in action

Attention is the mechanism that lets a model decide, for each word, which other words in the sentence matter most to its meaning. In "The cat sat on the mat because it was tired," attention connects "it" to "cat" — not "mat." This selective focus is what makes modern AI so capable.

Key Points

  • Attention is learnt, not programmed
  • "Self-attention" means every word attends to every other word simultaneously
  • Real models have multiple attention heads running in parallel
  • This is the core innovation of the transformer architecture (2017)

Attention works at one level. But LLMs stack many layers of this — each one understanding at a different depth.
