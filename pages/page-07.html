Attention: What Relates to What

Selective focus — the mechanism that made the transformer work.

In "The cat sat on the mat because it was tired," attention connects it to cat — not mat. That selective focus is learnt, not programmed. Nobody wrote a rule; the model discovered the relationship by predicting text at massive scale.

Key concepts

Learnt, not programmed emerges from exposure to text
Self-attention all words attend to all words
Multiple heads in parallel different heads, different patterns
The transformer (2017) the core architectural innovation
