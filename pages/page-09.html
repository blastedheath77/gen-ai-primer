Training: Learning at Scale

From random guessing to remarkable — through trillions of tiny corrections.

Training starts knowing nothing — predicting randomly. After trillions of tokens, the patterns absorbed are so rich that coherent text, answers, and code emerge — all as a consequence of getting better at one task: predict the next token.

Key concepts

Nobody programs knowledge it emerges from exposure to data
Iterative adjustment millions of small gradient steps
Scale = capability emergent jumps at key thresholds
Why training is expensive GPUs, months, millions of dollars
