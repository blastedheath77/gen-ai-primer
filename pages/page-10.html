Temperature: The Creativity Dial

Controlling randomness — from deterministic to surprising.

Temperature controls how the model samples from its probability distribution. It doesn't change what the model knows — it changes how it chooses. This single dial underpins the mechanical basis of "creativity" in AI.

Key concepts

Low = predictable deterministic, safe, consistent
High = varied diverse, surprising, sometimes wild
Changes choice, not knowledge sampling method, not capabilities
Task-dependent setting code low, creativity high
