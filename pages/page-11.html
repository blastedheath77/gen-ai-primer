Prompting: Shaping the Output

Your words, their influence

Because an LLM generates by continuing a sequence, the text you provide — your prompt — fundamentally shapes what follows. A vague prompt gives vague results. A specific, structured prompt guides the model precisely. Prompting isn't a trick; it's the primary interface between human intent and model behaviour.

Key Points

  • Prompting is a skill, not a gimmick
  • The model has no hidden understanding of what you "really mean"
  • Specificity wins: the more precise your prompt, the more useful the output
  • Five techniques (audience, format, persona, constraints, examples) cover ~90% of everyday prompting needs

You've been writing instructions. But LLMs can do something remarkable: learn new tasks from just a few examples, right there in the prompt.
