Prompting: Shaping the Output

Your words are the primary interface between intent and model behaviour.

Because an LLM generates by continuing a sequence, the text you provide fundamentally shapes what follows. A vague prompt gives vague results. A specific, structured prompt guides the model precisely — not by magic, by mechanics.

Key concepts

Vague in, vague out precision of prompt = precision of output
Prompting is a skill learnable, not mysterious
Audience + format who reads it and how it's structured
5 core techniques audience, format, persona, constraints, examples
