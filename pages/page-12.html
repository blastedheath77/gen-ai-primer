In-Context Learning

Show, don't just tell — and the model generalises without retraining.

Show the model three examples of a pattern in your prompt, and it attempts a fourth — no retraining required. Nobody designed this; it emerged when models reached sufficient scale. It's one of the most powerful and surprising properties of LLMs.

Key concepts

Emergent ability not designed — appeared at scale
Generalise from examples pattern → extension, no weight update
0-shot, 1-shot, few-shot more examples helps — up to a point
Still just prediction examples shape context, not weights
