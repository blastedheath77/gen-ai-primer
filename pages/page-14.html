Alignment: Steering the Model

Teaching AI to be helpful — through human feedback, not rules.

A raw LLM trained only on prediction will also produce toxic, harmful, or misleading content — because those patterns exist in training data. Alignment steers the model toward helpful, honest, and harmless behaviour by learning what humans prefer.

Key concepts

Human feedback loop RLHF — learning from human ratings
Shifts probabilities suppresses, doesn't erase
Ongoing, not one-time continuous evaluation and re-tuning
Over-alignment tradeoff too cautious = too useless
