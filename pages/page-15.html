Beyond Text: Images, Audio, and Code

The universal prediction engine

You've seen how a transformer predicts the next word in a sentence. But the same trick works on anything that can be broken into a sequence. Images become grid patches. Audio becomes spectral slices. Even combinations of text and images get merged into one token stream. The mechanism is always the same: predict the next piece from context.

Key Points

  • "Generative AI" isn't just text — the same principles power image generators, music AI, code assistants
  • Tokenisation is the universal interface: any data that can be broken into pieces can be modelled
  • The transformer's power is domain-agnostic — it's a general-purpose sequence prediction machine
  • AI progress in one domain (text) often transfers to others (images, audio, code)
