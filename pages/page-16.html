Real-World Applications

AI across every domain

Generative AI isn't one tool — it's a capability that reshapes work across every field. Understanding the mechanism (which you now do) lets you evaluate which applications are solid, which are overhyped, and where the real opportunities lie.

Key Points

  • AI capability varies enormously by application
  • "Green" tasks are ones where the prediction mechanism naturally excels (text transformation, summarisation, ideation)
  • "Red" tasks involve factual claims or high-stakes decisions where hallucination is dangerous
  • The best use of AI is typically collaborative: human expertise + AI capability

AI can do impressive things. But it also has real limits and raises genuine ethical questions. Let's be honest about both.
