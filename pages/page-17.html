Limitations, Bias & Responsible Use

The honest conversation

Generative AI has structural limitations — not just current weaknesses that'll be fixed in the next version, but fundamental constraints that follow from how the mechanism works. Understanding them is as important as understanding the capabilities.

Key Points

  • Knowledge cutoff means the model literally cannot know about events after training
  • Bias isn't just a PR problem — it's a structural consequence of learning from human-generated text
  • "Pattern matching" is extremely powerful but not equivalent to logical reasoning
  • Each limitation maps directly to the mechanism
  • Responsible use means knowing when to trust, when to verify, and when not to use AI at all
